Budae Jjigae
"Army Base Stew"

Budae-jjigae (부대찌개; literally "army base stew") or spicy sausage stew is a type of Korean jjigae (stew), made with ham, sausage, spam, baked beans, kimchi, and gochujang. The dish was created shortly after the armistice that ended the Korean War, using the scrounged or smuggled surplus foods from U.S. military bases.[2] Although the dish was born in the period of post-war impoverishment, it continued to be popular during the period of rapid growth, and is still popular today. There are many restaurants specializing in budae-jjigae; the most famous ones on the budae-jjigae street in Uijeongbu, where the dish was first made. The dish is now a popular anju (accompaniment to alcoholic drinks) and a lunch item. Also, there is a franchise brand that specializes in budae-jjigae. There is "Nolbu Budae-Jjigae" as a representative franchise store.

Ingredients(for 2 or 3 persons)

  • Chorizo : 1 each or more
  • spam : 1 tin
  • gochujang : 2 Table spoon
  • minced garlic : 1 Table spoon
  • sugar : 1 Table spoon
  • Kimchi : 1 cup or 1 handful size
  • spring onion : 1/3 bunch
  • sliced cheese : 1 or 2 sheets
  • chilli powder : 2 Table spoon
  • Soy sauce(Jin Ganjang) : 3 Table spoon
  • water : 700ml
  • brown onion : 1/2 each
  • minced beef or bacon : a handful
  • instant ramen noodle : 1 each
  • baked bean : one small tin (approx. 100g)

Method

  • Blench Spam and Chorizo in boiling water in 1 min
  • Slice spam,Chorizo and brown onion
  • put water in the wide and shallow casserole pot
  • put gochujang, chilli powder, garlic, soy sauce and sugar in the pot
  • put the pot on the stove and turn it in high heat
  • when the broth is boiling, put kimchi, mined beef.
  • put Chorizo, sapm, onion and baked beans
  • put instant ramen noodle and cook it. it takes couple of mins
  • serve it with garnish of shredded spring onion
  • you and enjoy it with bowl of rice
